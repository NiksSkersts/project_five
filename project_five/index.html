More About Me. [Out of Date]

Ever since I was a child, I loved playing video games like Mount&Blade Warband or Minecraft.

I wasn't particularly good at them, but I loved playing them because they allowed me to be myself.

While my friends made magnificent castles, I made a hut. I kept adding and removing stuff until it was perfect, and when it was perfect, I kept adding more to make it more perfect.

Even though I stopped playing video games, the wish or the need to create my place still exists.

This website, in a way, is like my house in Minecraft. As long as I draw my breath, I have the potential to add the things I love, remove the things I don't need, and upgrade the stuff that hasn't aged well. That's what I plan to do.

Nonetheless, thank you for taking interest in me and my website!
